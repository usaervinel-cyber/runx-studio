About the studio

We build websites you're not ashamed to show a partner,
an investor, and your own mom.

Who we are

A team of 32+ — designers, developers, strategists, marketers, AI specialists, product and project managers. Founded in 2016 around a simple idea: the web should work. No templates, no 'done in three days', no 'it's impossible'.

How we work

Strict fixed-scope contracts with a deadline. Weekly check-ins on Loom. Full access to Figma and the repo. The client knows what's happening every day of the week. No black boxes.

Stack

Next.js · React · Framer Motion · GSAP · Three.js · Sanity · Framer · Tailwind · Vercel · Cloudflare · Figma · After Effects · Runway ML.

What we don't do

Templates. WordPress on Elementor. 'SEO-stuffing' for search engines. Work for pizza. Projects where the only KPI is 'just have one'. If the site has no goal — we'd both be sad together.

Process · 04 steps

From first call
to launch — 7–14 days.

01

Brief & Strategy

A 60-minute call plus a questionnaire. We walk out with an understanding of your audience, competitors, and KPIs. You get a PDF brief with site architecture and behavior scenarios.

3—5 days
02

UX + Moodboard

Wireframes in Figma plus three visual directions. We pick together. We show references and explain why this particular rhythm will work for your segment.

5—7 days
03

Design + Animation

Final design in Figma, motion prototype in Framer. We show every interactive state before development — you know exactly what you'll get.

2—3 weeks
04

Dev & Launch

Development, integrations (CRM, Analytics, forms), real-device QA, SEO basics. Full ongoing support after launch on a monthly subscription — updates, fixes, growth experiments.

1—2 weeks
